# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 15, 2024**

## Introduction

Menkiki ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Camera Access
- We access your device's camera solely to capture photos of food for identification purposes
- Photos are processed locally on your device using AI technology
- **We do not store, upload, or share your photos** with any servers or third parties

### Location Data
- We request access to your location to find nearby restaurants
- Location data is used only when you actively search for restaurants
- Your location is sent to Google Places API to retrieve restaurant information
- **We do not store or track your location history**

### Local Storage
- Search history is stored locally on your device
- Favorite restaurants are stored locally on your device
- This data never leaves your device and can be deleted at any time within the app

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Google Places API
- Used to find restaurants near your location
- Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- We only send your coordinates to retrieve nearby restaurant data

### Expo (Development Platform)
- Used to build and distribute the app
- Subject to [Expo's Privacy Policy](https://expo.dev/privacy)

## Data Retention

- **Photos**: Not retained. Processed in real-time and immediately discarded
- **Location**: Not retained. Used only for the current search session
- **Search History**: Stored locally until you delete it
- **Favorites**: Stored locally until you delete them

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data (all stored locally on your device)
- **Delete** your data at any time through the app settings
- **Deny** camera or location permissions (app functionality will be limited)

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
