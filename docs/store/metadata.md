## Description

### English

Hungry? Just snap a photo of the food you're craving and let Menkiki find the best nearby restaurants for you!

**How it works:**
1. Take a photo of any food (pizza, sushi, burger, ramen, empanadas...)
2. Our AI instantly recognizes what you're craving
3. Get a curated list of nearby restaurants serving that dish
4. Call or navigate directly to your chosen restaurant

**Features:**
- AI-powered food recognition using machine learning
- Find restaurants based on what you're craving, not just keywords
- Save your favorite spots for quick access
- View your search history
- Works offline with cached data
- Dark mode for comfortable nighttime browsing

**Perfect for:**
- Foodies exploring new cuisines
- Travelers looking for local gems
- Anyone who knows what they want to eat but not where to find it

Download Menkiki and let your cravings guide you to your next great meal!
