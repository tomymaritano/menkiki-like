# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 2024**

## Introduction

Menkiki ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Camera Access
- We request camera access to take photos of food for classification
- Photos are processed locally on your device using machine learning
- **Photos are NOT uploaded to any server**
- Photos are temporarily stored only for the duration of the classification process

### Location Data
- We request location access to find nearby restaurants
- Location data is used only to query restaurant APIs
- We do not store or track your location history on our servers

### Local Storage
- Favorite restaurants (stored locally on your device)
- Search history (stored locally on your device)
- Onboarding completion status (stored locally on your device)

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Account credentials
- Payment information
- Browsing history outside the app
- Photos (they remain on your device)
- Precise location history

## Third-Party Services

### Google Places API
- When you search for restaurants, we query the Google Places API
- Google's privacy policy applies to this data: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### TensorFlow (On-Device)
- Food classification is performed entirely on your device
- No image data is sent to external servers for classification

## Data Security

- All data processing happens locally on your device
- We use industry-standard security practices
- No sensitive data is transmitted to external servers

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ (iOS) / Everyone (Android).

## Your Rights

You can:
- Clear your favorites and history at any time within the app
- Revoke camera and location permissions in your device settings
- Delete the app to remove all locally stored data

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: privacy@menkiki.app
