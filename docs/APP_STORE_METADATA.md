### Full Description

**Craving something delicious? Let AI be your guide.**

Menkiki is the smartest way to discover where to eat. Simply take a photo of any food — from a magazine, social media, or walking down the street — and our AI instantly identifies it and shows you the best restaurants nearby serving that exact dish.

**How it works:**
• Snap a photo of any food you're craving
• Our AI chef identifies it in seconds
• Discover top-rated restaurants nearby
• Save your favorites for later

**Features:**
• AI-powered food recognition
• Real-time restaurant discovery
• Distance and ratings at a glance
• Save favorites with one tap
• Beautiful dark mode interface
• Works completely offline for AI detection

**Perfect for:**
• Foodies exploring new cuisines
• Travelers in unfamiliar cities
• Anyone wondering "where can I get that?"

Download Menkiki and never wonder where to eat again.
